Quick Start Rules

Everything you need to start playing in 5 minutes

1

Pick Your Cards

Each player chooses 1-3 NWG cards to form their party. Higher rarity = stronger character. Mix roles for a balanced team.

2

Convert Stats

Use our converter tool or the quick formula: ATK÷5 = STR, DEF÷5 = CON, SPD÷5 = DEX. Rarity sets your level (Common=3, Legendary=12).

3

Roll to Act

When you try something risky, roll d20 + relevant modifier. Beat the target number (usually 10-20) to succeed.

4

Track HP

Damage reduces HP. At 0 HP, you're knocked out. Allies can revive you with healing or potions. Full rest restores all HP.

5

Use Abilities

Special card abilities = once per battle spells. "Guild Buff" might give +2 to ally rolls. DM interprets effects.

6

Earn Rewards

Completing quests earns "Sacred Logs" (XP). 10 logs = level up. Find rare items, unlock new abilities, become legends.
