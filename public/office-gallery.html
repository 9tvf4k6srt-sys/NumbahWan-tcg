Brand consistency rule. Cast-brass PINFORGE emblem (twin pillars + N-wedge), 30cm, off-center, on raw masonry — identical on both branches. Cinnabar wax-seal on folios — identical. Camera grammar (Phase One IQ4 / Leica Q3 35mm, f/4, Portra 400 grain) — identical.
What changes. Taipei walnut → SG Burmese teak. Taipei oxidized-brass furniture detailing → SG black powder-coated steel (brass reserved for the emblem). Taipei lime plaster → SG board-formed concrete. Taipei travertine → SG polished concrete + limestone. Taipei steel-mullion-feel curtain wall → SG louver-shaded curtain wall. Taipei dry-warm side light → SG monsoon-variant grey-green diffuse light (2 of 6 frames).
