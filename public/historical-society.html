About the Society

The NumbahWan Historical Society was established in 2024 to preserve, document, and study the rich heritage of the NumbahWan Guild. Our mission encompasses the management of the Fashion Museum, oversight of the Guild Vault archives, and publication of scholarly research on digital artifact preservation.
