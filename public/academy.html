12-Week Curriculum

Transform from beginner to Prompt Wizard in just 12 weeks with our structured, hands-on curriculum.

Week 1-2

Introduction to AI Friends

Meet AI assistants, understand what they can do, and learn basic English conversation with AI.

What is AI? ChatGPT Basics Polite Prompting
Week 3-4

The Art of Asking Questions

Learn how to ask clear, specific questions. Master the 5W1H (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How).

5W1H Framework Specific vs Vague Context Giving
Week 5-6

Creative Storytelling with AI

Create amazing stories, poems, and adventures by learning advanced prompting techniques.

Story Prompts Character Creation Plot Building
Week 7-8

AI Art & Image Generation

Learn to describe images in English and create amazing AI art with tools like DALL-E and Midjourney.

Descriptive English Art Vocabulary Style & Mood
Week 9-10

Problem Solving with AI

Use AI as a learning tool! Get help with homework, learn new topics, and solve puzzles together.

Research Skills Fact Checking Learning Strategies
Week 11-12

Final Project: AI Showcase

Create your own AI project! Build a storybook, chatbot persona, or art collection to present.

Project Planning Presentation Skills Graduation!
