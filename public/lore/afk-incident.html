The Aftermath

#4. Not #1. Not even top 3. But considering they missed 75% of the event, it was a miracle.

The guild that won? They had 12 hours of damage. NumbahWan did it in 2.

Per-hour damage rate? NumbahWan was actually #1.

Secret #4 of 5

The New Guild Rule

After The Great AFK Incident, a new rule was added to the guild charter:

"No 'strategy sessions' within 24 hours of major events. Anyone who says 'easy win' is immediately muted."

The rule has been broken three times since then.

Epilogue

The Legend Lives On

Every year, on the anniversary of The Great AFK Incident, NumbahWan holds a memorial event.

Members share their "what I was dreaming about during the raid" stories. Prizes are given for the most ridiculous excuses.

And at exactly 10:00 AM server time, everyone types "zzz" in chat. Then they actually play the game.

The True Legacy

NumbahWan? Still here. Still together. Still occasionally falling asleep during raids.

Turns out the guild that knows how to laugh at itself is the guild that lasts.
