Chapter 1: Day One (The Worst Decision)

It was a rainy Tuesday when a player who would become known as RegginA first logged into the server. The username selection screen blinked at them. They had exactly 30 seconds to choose a name that would define their digital existence for the next several years. What happened next would become the stuff of legend— or as HR keeps calling it, "Exhibit A."

Editor's Note: We've received 47 cease-and-desist letters about this name. RegginA insists it's just their actual name spelled backwards. Their actual name? ...We're still investigating.

The first three hours of gameplay were... eventful. RegginA managed to: die to a tutorial slime (twice), accidentally sell their starter weapon, and somehow challenge the server's #1 ranked player to a duel. The duel lasted 0.3 seconds. The humiliation lasted considerably longer.

"I knew from that moment I would either become the greatest player this server has ever seen, or I would burn it all down trying. Spoiler: it's been a bit of both." — RegginA, Day 1 Journal Entry (recovered from a suspicious USB drive)

What followed was a period historians now call "The Unhinged Era." RegginA discovered that while they lacked conventional skill, they possessed something far more dangerous: absolutely zero regard for sleep schedules.

Chapter 3: The Rise to Power

By 2022, NumbahWan had grown from a joke guild to... a slightly less funny joke guild with actual power. The secret? RegginA had discovered something profound: most people will follow anyone who acts confident enough while spending enough money.

Fun fact: RegginA's actual combat skill ranking puts them at approximately #847 on the server. Their SPENDING ranking? Let's just say the leaderboard doesn't go that high.

The guild's philosophy became clear: "We may not be the most skilled, but we are definitely the most... present." Members describe the experience as "Stockholm Syndrome, but make it fun."

"RegginA once kept us in a boss fight for 6 hours because they were 'sure we could get it next try.' We did not get it. But somehow, I'd do it again. What has this guild done to me?" — Anonymous Guild Member, currently in therapy

Chapter 4: The Legend Solidifies

Today, RegginA presides over NumbahWan like a benevolent dictator who read exactly one chapter of a leadership book and then got distracted by a gacha banner. The guild has become a force of nature— unpredictable, occasionally destructive, but undeniably entertaining.

When asked about their legacy, RegginA's response was characteristically humble: "I just wanted to play a game. Somehow I created a cult. I'd apologize but honestly? This is the funniest thing that's ever happened to me."

Current guild status: 200+ active members, 3 spin-off guilds, 1 pending lawsuit (unrelated to gaming), and an HR department that definitely shouldn't exist for a gaming organization.
SECRET #3: The Name Mystery... Solved?
Click for the REAL reason behind the name...
After years of speculation, RegginA finally admitted in a 3 AM Discord rant that the name is... actually just a typo. They meant to type "Regina" (their cat's name) but were so sleep-deprived they hit the keys in the wrong order. By the time they noticed, they'd already spent $50 on starter packs and refused to make a new account "out of principle." The cat, Regina, has since passed away. Her memory lives on in the most chaotic way possible.
