Chapter 1

A Log Among Legends

In a world of legendary swords, mythical armor, and ancient relics... NumbahWan chose to worship a log.

Not a magical log. Not a log imbued with the power of ancient gods. Just a regular, ordinary, everyday piece of wood that somehow became the most valuable item in the entire guild.

This is the story of how a worthless item became priceless.

"Some guilds collect rare weapons. Some hoard gold. We worship firewood. And honestly? We wouldn't have it any other way."
— Guild Charter, Article 7

The First Log

The Sacred Log was RegginA's first item drop. Not her first rare. Not her first legendary. Her literal first item from killing a slime on Day 1.

The item description read: "A simple log. Worthless to most. But to someone, somewhere, it might mean everything."

Chapter 2

The Birth of a Currency

When NumbahWan needed a prestige currency—something that couldn't be bought, only earned through dedication—the answer was obvious.

Sacred Logs.

Unlike NWG which can be purchased, unlike Gold which flows freely from gameplay, the Sacred Log is earned only through achievement. Complete a card set. Reach the leaderboard. Find every secret in a deep dive.

It's the only currency in the system that measures one thing: devotion.

Why No Price?

The Sacred Log has no USD value. This was intentional.

"If you can buy devotion," RegginA once said, "then it isn't devotion. It's just another transaction."

The only way to get a Sacred Log is to earn it. Period.

Chapter 3

Powers of the Log

What can you do with a Sacred Log? Surprisingly... a lot.

  • Guaranteed Mythic Pull - 10 Logs for a guaranteed mythic card
  • Choose Your Legendary - 5 Logs to pick any legendary you want
  • Exclusive Merch Access - Some items can only be bought with Logs
  • VIP Status - Log holders get special recognition

But the real power? It's the meaning. When you see someone with Sacred Logs, you know they earned them.

The Log Ceremony

Every Sunday at midnight (server time), NumbahWan holds a Log Ceremony.

Members who earned their first Sacred Log that week are celebrated. Their names are read aloud. Screenshots are taken. It's ridiculous. It's beautiful. It's home.

Chapter 4

The Philosophy of Worth

The Sacred Log teaches one fundamental truth about value:

Worth is not inherent. Worth is assigned.

A diamond is just compressed carbon. A dollar is just paper. A legendary sword is just pixels. We give these things value because we collectively agree they have value.

NumbahWan decided that a log—the most worthless item in the game—would be their most valuable symbol. And by believing it, they made it true.

"The game developers probably think we're crazy. They gave us a trash item and we turned it into our religion. But that's the point, isn't it? We decide what matters."
— RegginA, explaining the Log to new members

The Log Vault

Deep within the guild's systems, there exists a Log Vault.

Every Sacred Log ever earned is tracked. Every ceremony recorded. If the guild ever disbands, the Vault data will be released as a permanent memorial to everyone who was ever part of NumbahWan.

Your Logs aren't just currency. They're your legacy.
