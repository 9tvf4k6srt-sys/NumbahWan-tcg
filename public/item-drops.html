BINDING SYSTEM

How Item Binding Works

In NWG, items are earned through gameplay. The binding system ensures fairness and prestige.

Account-Bound on Equip

Once you equip a Mythic or Legendary weapon/armor, it's permanently bound to your account. Cannot be traded, mailed, or sold. This is YOUR legacy. Mythic and Legendary combat gear falls under this category. If you earned it, you keep it forever.

Trade-Free Epic Items

Certain Epic-rarity fashion items, cosmetics, and collectibles are freely tradeable. Swap them with guildmates, sell them at the market, or gift them. These items carry no combat stats, only drip and prestige.

Zero Pay-to-Win

No item in this catalog can be purchased with real money. No loot boxes. No battle pass. No premium currency shortcut. Every single item drops from raids, events, and boss kills only. Your wallet is irrelevant here.

Mount & Pet Binding

Mounts and pets are account-bound on acquisition. The moment a mount drops, it's yours across all characters. Cannot be traded but can be displayed in your Trophy Hall for the entire server to envy.
