Arena Guide

NumbahWan TCG is a luck-based card battle game. Collect cards, pick your team, and let fate decide!

1

Collect Cards

Open packs at the Forge to get cards. Each card has a rarity from Common to Mythic.

  • Use Wood to open packs
  • Higher rarity = Higher luck bonus
  • Duplicate cards can be upgraded (star level)
2

Pick 3 Cards

In Battle Arena, select exactly 3 cards from your collection. Your cards' combined stats determine your Luck score.

  • Tap cards to select (max 3)
  • Choose difficulty (higher = bigger rewards)
  • Place your bet in NWG tokens
3

Battle & Win

Press BATTLE and let the RNG gods decide! Your luck score affects your win chance.

  • Win → Get rewards based on difficulty
  • Lose → Try again with encouragement
  • Streaks build up your luck meter!

Pro Tips

Start Easy

Begin with Easy difficulty to build up NWG tokens before trying harder battles.

Upgrade Cards

Duplicate cards can be upgraded to higher star levels, increasing their luck bonus.

Daily Wood

Collect daily rewards and complete tasks to earn Wood for more pack openings.

Hunt Mythics

Mythic cards (RegginA, Sacred Log) give massive luck bonuses. Keep pulling!

Luck Stacking Strategy

Combine 3 high-rarity cards for maximum luck. A team of Legendary + Legendary + Epic gives ~95% luck!

Don't Overbet

Even with high luck, you can still lose. Never bet more than you can afford to lose. This is gambling mechanics!

Currency Guide

Wood

Main currency for opening card packs at the Forge. Earn from daily login, tasks, and achievements.

NWG

Battle currency. Bet NWG to win more NWG. Start with free tokens when you join!

Gold

Premium currency for special packs and exclusive items. Rare and valuable!

Sacred Logs

Earned by burning duplicate cards. Use for card upgrades and special purchases.
