🎯 How to Win

🏰 Guild HP

Each player starts with 30 Guild HP. Reduce your opponent to 0 to claim victory!

💎 Energy System

Start with 1 Energy, gain +1 each turn (max 10). Energy fully refills every turn!

🃏 Deck Building

Build a deck of 20-30 cards. Balance members, moments, and gear for victory!

🏆 Strategy

Play cards strategically, manage your board, and outplay your opponent with skill + luck!

🃏 Card Types

👤 Members

Guild members that fight on the board. They have ATK, HP, and can attack each turn after being played.

⚡ Moments

Instant effects that trigger immediately and go to discard. The bread and butter of reactions!

⚔️ Gear

Equipment that attaches to members, boosting their stats permanently.

🌀 Vibes

Persistent effects that last 2-4 turns. Set the mood of the battlefield!

🔄 Turn Structure

🌅
1. START PHASE

Start-of-turn effects trigger. GRINDER cards gain +1/+1.

🎴
2. DRAW PHASE

Draw 1 card. Going second on turn 1? Draw 2!

💎
3. ENERGY PHASE

Gain +1 max energy (up to 10). Refill all energy.

🎮
4. MAIN PHASE

Play cards, use abilities, attach gear. No limit!

⚔️
5. COMBAT PHASE

Attack in SPD order. TAUNT must be attacked first. CRIT/DODGE rolls here!

🌙
6. END PHASE

End-of-turn effects. AFK cards heal. Discard to 10.
