🎯 How to Win

🏰 Guild HP

Each player starts with 30 Guild HP. Reduce your opponent to 0 to claim victory!

Alternative win: Opponent can't draw (deck out)

💎 Energy System

Start with 1 Energy, gain +1 each turn (max 10). Energy fully refills every turn!

Spend wisely - but don't hoard!

Sometimes the heart of the cards wins

🃏 Card Types

👤 Members

Guild members that fight on the board. They have ATK, HP, and can attack each turn after being played.

  • Can attack enemy members or Guild HP
  • Stay on board until destroyed (HP = 0)
  • Max 5 members on your board
  • Have summoning sickness (can't attack turn played)

⚡ Moments

Instant effects that trigger immediately and go to discard. The bread and butter of reactions!

  • Damage, healing, buffs, debuffs
  • Can be played anytime during your turn
  • Some have counter effects!
  • No HP - one-time use only

⚔️ Gear

Equipment that attaches to members, boosting their stats permanently.

  • Attach to any friendly member
  • Destroyed when member dies
  • Up to 2 gear per member
  • The Sacred Log is game-changing!

🌀 Vibes

Persistent effects that last 2-4 turns. Set the mood of the battlefield!

  • Affect the entire board
  • Can stack with other vibes
  • Up to 3 vibes active at once
  • "Tilt" is hilariously chaotic

📍 Spots

Locations that affect the entire board. Control the battlefield!

  • Only 1 spot can be active
  • Playing a new spot destroys the old one
  • Affects both players equally
  • AFK Spot = passive income

🔄 Turn Structure

Each turn follows these phases in order:

🌅
1. START PHASE
  • Start-of-turn effects trigger
  • Vibe durations tick down
  • GRINDER cards gain +1/+1
🎴
2. DRAW PHASE
  • Draw 1 card from your deck
  • Going second on turn 1? Draw 2 cards!
  • Empty deck = 1 FATIGUE damage (increases each turn)
💎
3. ENERGY PHASE
  • Gain +1 max energy (up to 10)
  • Refill ALL your energy to max
🎮
4. MAIN PHASE
  • Play cards from hand (spend energy)
  • Use member abilities
  • Attach gear to members
  • No limit on actions!
⚔️
5. COMBAT PHASE
  • Choose which members attack
  • Select targets (enemy members or Guild HP)
  • Attacks resolve in SPD order
  • TAUNT must be attacked first
  • CRIT and DODGE rolls happen here!
🌙
6. END PHASE
  • End-of-turn effects trigger
  • AFK cards regenerate HP
  • Discard down to 10 cards
  • Pass turn to opponent

🃏 Deck Building

📏 Deck Rules

  • Minimum: 20 cards
  • Maximum: 30 cards
  • Sweet Spot: 25 cards
  • Mythic/Legendary: 1 copy max
  • Epic/Rare: 2 copies max
  • Uncommon/Common: 3 copies max

⚖️ Recommended Ratios

  • Members: 12-16 cards (60%)
  • Moments: 4-8 cards (25%)
  • Gear: 2-4 cards (10%)
  • Vibes/Spots: 2-4 cards (5%)

📈 Energy Curve

  • 1-2 cost: 6-8 cards (early game)
  • 3-4 cost: 8-10 cards (midgame core)
  • 5-6 cost: 4-6 cards (power plays)
  • 7+ cost: 2-4 cards (finishers)
✅ Deck Building Tips
  • Include a good energy curve - don't go all expensive cards!
  • Have at least one TAUNT to protect key cards
  • Include 2-3 removal moments for emergencies
  • Don't go all-in on RNG cards unless you're making content 😏
  • Test your deck against aggro, control, and combo matchups
