About This Coin

The inaugural coin of the Kintsugi Gold Collection. About This Coin

The most accessible entry to the Kintsugi Gold Collection. The qilin — the benevolent mythical creature said to appear only in times of great peace — stands upon cracked golden earth, with kintsugi veins running through the ground beneath its hooves. The reverse features floating pottery fragments being drawn together by streams of liquid gold, symbolizing that what is broken becomes more beautiful. This quarter-ounce coin uses a unique satin proof finish combining frosted relief with a brushed-matte field, giving it a distinctly warm lustre. Minted July 2025 in the initial batch of 5,888 pieces. Supplied in a padded blue suede clamshell with numbered holographic certificate and assay card.
