Artifact Overview

In a world of cool goggles and intimidating masks, the Tree Branch Nose stands as a testament to MapleStory's willingness to embrace the absurd. This bizarre face accessory transforms any character into a wooden-nosed wonder.

Inspired by the classic tale of Pinocchio, this item gained cult status among players who appreciated its humorous aesthetic. It became the unofficial symbol of players who didn't take the game—or themselves—too seriously.

Forest Origins

Ellinia Forest Research Archives

According to in-game lore (and player imagination), the Tree Branch Nose was first discovered when a young magician in Ellinia Forest fell asleep under an ancient oak. When she woke, she found a perfectly branch-shaped piece of wood had somehow attached to her face.

Rather than being horrified, she found the look... endearing. Word spread, and soon adventurers across Maple World were actively seeking out the Stumps and Tree monsters that might drop this peculiar accessory.

"I've been asked why we designed such a silly item. The answer is simple: because games should make people smile. Not every item needs to look cool—some just need to be fun."

— Original Wizet Artist, 2004

Meme Evolution

"Lies Detected"

Players would equip the nose and spam chat with "My nose is growing!" whenever someone made dubious claims about their damage numbers or item drops.

Peak Era: 2005-2008

Guild Photo Chaos

There was always that one guild member who would switch to the Tree Branch Nose right before official guild photos, ruining the "serious" aesthetic.

Still Happening: Present

Anti-Fashion Fashion

A counter-culture emerged: players who deliberately wore the ugliest items possible as a statement against NX whale culture. The Tree Branch Nose was their crown jewel.

Movement: 2010-2015

Boss Run Intimidation

Some players swore that wearing the Tree Branch Nose to boss fights was a power move— asserting such confidence that fashion didn't matter. Results varied.

Effectiveness: Questionable

Did You Know?

The Tree Branch Nose was originally designed as a joke item that was never supposed to be released. A developer accidentally left it in a patch build, and player response was so positive that it became a permanent addition.

— Unverified but widely believed community lore
