On the spot
01

Snap any space with your phone

No 3D modeling. No SketchUp. No Revit. Just take a photo. Our AI instantly creates a depth map, identifies every surface (walls, floor, ceiling, windows), and detects existing furniture. Works on any phone — LiDAR-enhanced on iPhone Pro.

⚡ AI analysis in under 5 seconds
📷 → 🗺
02

Place real furniture at real scale

Browse curated catalogs from manufacturers like Herman Miller, West Elm, and Restoration Hardware. Every item has real dimensions, real materials, and real pricing. Place them in the scene with correct perspective — the AI handles the spatial math.

🛒 Total cost updates in real-time
🛒 → 🏠
03

Render at the fidelity you need

Three quality modes: Sketch (5 seconds, conceptual), Concept (15 seconds, mid-fidelity), and Presentation (60 seconds, ultra-realistic 4K). Start with a sketch, refine with the client, finalize in presentation mode. Controllable realism — never over-promise.

🎨 From sketch to 4K in your pocket
✏️ → 🎨 → 📸
04

Share, iterate, close

Show the render to your client on your phone. They want walnut instead of oak? Change it, re-render in 15 seconds. When they're happy, share an interactive link, generate a PDF proposal, or capture approval — all from the app. No more losing clients to the 7-day gap.

✅ Same-day proposals, same-day approvals
