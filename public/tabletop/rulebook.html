Core Philosophy

The Golden Rule

Fun First, Rules Second. If a rule gets in the way of an awesome moment, ignore it. The DM's word is final, but the DM's job is to make everyone have a blast.

The d20 System

Everything uses a single d20 die:

  • Roll d20 + Modifier vs Target Number
  • 10 = Easy (jumping a small gap)
  • 15 = Medium (picking a basic lock)
  • 20 = Hard (convincing a guard to let you pass)
  • 25+ = Legendary (only possible with high stats)
Pro Tip: The Rule of Cool

If a player describes something awesome, give them advantage (roll 2d20, take higher). Creativity should be rewarded!

Card → Character Conversion

The Conversion Formula

Your NWG card stats translate directly to D&D ability scores:

Card Stat D&D Ability Formula Example (80 ATK)
ATK (Attack) Strength (STR) ATK ÷ 5 16 STR (+3 modifier)
DEF (Defense) Constitution (CON) DEF ÷ 5 16 CON (+3 modifier)
SPD (Speed) Dexterity (DEX) SPD ÷ 5 16 DEX (+3 modifier)
Special Ability INT/WIS/CHA DM assigns based on ability type Varies

Quick Start

Don't have your cards memorized? Just pick a rarity and assign: STR 14, DEX 12, CON 14, INT 10, WIS 12, CHA 10 as baseline. Adjust +2/-2 based on card type!

Combat Rules

Initiative (Who Goes First)

At the start of combat:

  • Everyone rolls d20 + DEX modifier
  • Highest goes first, then descending order
  • Ties? Higher DEX wins. Still tied? Roll off!

Your Turn (3 Actions)

On your turn, you can do:

  • Move - Up to 30 feet (6 squares)
  • Action - Attack, Cast Spell, Use Item, Help ally
  • Bonus Action - Quick ability, second attack (if you have it)

Attacking

To attack an enemy:

  • Roll d20 + STR (melee) or DEX (ranged)
  • If you beat their AC (Armor Class), you hit!
  • Damage: Roll weapon die + STR/DEX modifier
  • Natural 20: CRITICAL HIT! Double damage dice!
  • Natural 1: Critical miss. Something funny happens.

Defense (Armor Class)

Your AC = 10 + DEX modifier + armor bonus

  • No Armor: +0
  • Light Armor: +2
  • Medium Armor: +4
  • Heavy Armor: +6
  • Shield: +2 extra
Speed Up Combat

Roll attack and damage at the same time! "I swing my sword... 17 to hit, 8 damage if it lands!"

Abilities & Spells

Card Abilities = Spells

Your NWG card's special ability becomes a usable spell:

  • Use once per battle (short rest recharges)
  • DM interprets the effect based on ability name
  • More powerful = requires higher rarity

Common Ability Translations

Card Ability D&D Effect
"Guild Buff" All allies get +2 to rolls for 1 round
"Critical Strike" Next attack is automatically a crit
"Healing Touch" Heal ally for 2d8 + your level HP
"Taunt" Enemy must attack you for 1 round
"Stealth Mode" Become invisible for 1 round
"AoE Blast" Deal 3d6 damage to all enemies in 20ft

Level Up System

Sacred Log XP

Characters level up by earning Sacred Logs (in-game XP):

  • 10 Sacred Logs = Level Up!
  • +1 to any ability score
  • +10 HP
  • +1 Spell Slot
  • Unlock new ability at levels 5, 10, 15

DM Quick Guide

Being a Good DM

  • Say "Yes, and..." - Build on player ideas
  • Keep it Moving - If rules slow you down, make a ruling and move on
  • Everyone Gets Spotlight - Rotate focus between players
  • Describe with Senses - What do they see, hear, smell?
  • Let Them Fail Forward - Failed rolls should create new opportunities

Building Encounters

Match enemy difficulty to party level:

  • Easy: Enemy HP = Party average HP × 0.5
  • Medium: Enemy HP = Party average HP × 1
  • Hard: Enemy HP = Party average HP × 1.5
  • Boss: Enemy HP = Total party HP combined
First Time DM?

Use the Zakum's Return campaign - it's designed for new DMs with pre-built encounters, dialogue options, and maps!
