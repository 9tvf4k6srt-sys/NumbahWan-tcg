This content is MATHEMATICALLY IMPOSSIBLE to solo. Just like EverQuest's forced grouping, you need a balanced party. No tank? You'll be one-shot. No healer? Attrition kills you. No DPS? The timer runs out. This is the Golden Age way.

Death Penalties (Loss Aversion)

Like EverQuest's legendary corpse runs: death is not trivial. You LOSE things. This makes every battle meaningful and every survival a triumph.
