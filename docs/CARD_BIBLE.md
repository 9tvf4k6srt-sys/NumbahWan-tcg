## 🎮 THE WORLD

### Setting
A chaotic guild in a fantasy MMO world where the line between game and reality blurs. Members aren't just players - they're legends, memes, and disasters.

### The NumbahWan Guild
RegginA founded NumbahWan after rage-quitting her previous guild over loot drama. She recruited the weirdest, most dedicated, most unhinged players she could find. Together they're either the strongest guild in the realm... or the biggest circus. Depends on the day.

### Sacred Artifacts
- **The Sacred Log** - Guild's mystical artifact, source of all card power
- **The Forge** - Where cards manifest from pure grind energy
- **The Leaderboard** - Eternal competition that drives everything

## 🎨 ART STYLE GUIDE

### By Rarity
- **Mythic**: Full realistic fantasy, detailed, epic lighting
- **Legendary**: Realistic with personality, memorable poses
- **Epic**: Semi-realistic, dynamic action, expressive
- **Rare**: Stylized realistic, clear silhouettes
- **Uncommon**: Semi-chibi, big expressions, meme-ready
- **Common**: Chibi, simple, cute, icon-like

### Consistent Elements
- Guild crest (log symbol) visible on members
- Role color coding in designs
- Expressions that tell a story
- Modern gaming references in fantasy setting
